Technology Leadership in TESOL

Purpose & Goals

Welcome to Technology Leadership in TESOL! A technology leadership and knowledge resource for TESOL instructors. As technology changes, the implementation of new modes of digital learning and information gathering in the classroom change as well. The purpose of this website is to educate instructors in the TESOL field about how to advance as technology leaders in the adult ESL classroom.

The goals for what you can learn on this site are as follows:

  1. Describe the connection between adult education in TESOL and sociotechnical and sociocultural definitions.

  2. Describe meaningful technology integration and its application in sociotechnical second language contexts.

  3. Analyze examples of technology use in second language contexts and evaluate whether they are meaningful or not.
